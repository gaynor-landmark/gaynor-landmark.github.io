I am at the beginning of a new adventure. After many years of drifting peacefully along on a gently flowing river, I rounded a bend, and there were rapids ahead! Too late to make for the bank, there was only the option to ride the rapids and hope to make it to where the river runs gently again. But for now, I must keep paddling.

Attending EDA is my new adventure and I intend to learn as much as possible and hopefully enjoy the ride. I expect it to be challenging, but with challenges come victories and growth. I am so grateful for this opportunity. There is a new adventure around every bend in the river!
