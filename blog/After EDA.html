EDA - an awesome experience

So what was it like?

My head is still spinning! It was an intensive 9 weeks, in a focused immersive environment, designed to encourage accelerated learning. The course pushed me beyond what I thought I was capable of, while supporting my learning in practical ways. There was both theoretical and practical learning, with lots of pair-programming and group project experience. What did I get out if it? Well, apart from the obvious technical skills, and some life-long friends, I re-discovered my love of programming. After working for one company for a number of years, it was so refreshing to learn new ways of doing things and spend time discovering new technolgies. I was greedy to learn and I was encouraged to endulge myself!
