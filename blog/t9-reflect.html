Phase-0 Reflection

Which concepts did you find most difficult?

The Javascript is definitely the most challenging at this stage. 'Closures' are a new concept for me - but I can see how they could be useful.

Which skills/concepts do you feel still confused by?

I am still a bit confused by which technology to use where, and sorting out which syntax is used for each is still something I have to look up, rather than it being on my finger tips. I can't say that I am confused by touch-typing, but it has been far more of a challenge to me than I expected. Years of bad habits, I guess!

Which skills/concepts do you feel you understand best?

I feel that I have a basic understanding of most concepts - still a long way to go to become proficient.

Which aspects of web development so far do you enjoy the most?

I'm loving it all - just wish I was better at it.
