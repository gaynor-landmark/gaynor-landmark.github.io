A New Beginning

Espiral Dev Academy - a new beginning

I am at the beginning of a new adventure. After many years of drifting peacefully along on a gently flowing river, I rounded a bend, and there were rapids ahead! Was it too late to make for the bank? For now, I have chosen to head out to deeper water by turning down a safe and boring job. I am expecting hard work, but an opening up to new ideas, technology and creativity.
Attending EDA is my new adventure and I intend to learn as much as possible and hopefully enjoy the ride. I expect it to be challenging, but with challenges come victories and growth. I am so grateful for this opportunity. There is an adventure around every bend in the river!
